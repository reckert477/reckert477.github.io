Overview

Submission for Global Game Jam 2019: Home
The concept of Doodly Pets came from the Looking Glass and Leapmotion, and seeing what we would be able to do with these technologies. The Looking Glass, a holographic display, reminded us much of a self enclosed room, which inspired us to draw from the classic Tamagotchi series, but with the ability to craft your own pet and have a hand in designing their envoronmnent.

Challenge(s)

Doodly Pets largely ran into the issue of being a new technology worked on by a small team.
As development software for the looking glass only ran on Windows, that immediately put a restriction on how many people could be working with the leapmotion at once.
