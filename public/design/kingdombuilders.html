Overview

In Twitchchat, anonymous imageboards, Reddit, and large online communities, there is always the issue of member involvement and engagement. In the context of Twitch streams in particular, the streamer is almost always looking for more engagment and better sense of community. In actuality, there are always more viewers than people who interact. Thus the question is how can we use game as a means to pull more people from viewers into interacters. Kingdom Builders is our solution to that question, being an idle game that operates as an add-on to a Twitch chanel.

Challenge(s)

Despite being a fundamentally simple concept, many factors had to be taken into consideration due to the varied interests of people playing this game, both on the streamer as well as chat side.
The most interesting challenge of Kingdom Builder is that the game has to be a distinctly secondary experience, which is seemingly at odds with being fun, engaging, and everything else a game typically wants to be.
